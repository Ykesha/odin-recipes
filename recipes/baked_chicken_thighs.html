Baked Chicken Thighs

Description

Chicken is one of the most popular and versitile meats. There are plenty of ways to enjoy and prepare it.

Baked Chicken thighs are both juicy and delicious. And best of all they are easy to prepare.

Ingredients

    1 lb. of Chicken
    Olive Oil
    Salt
    Pepper
    Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven at 400 degrees.
  2. Remove Chicken from packaging and dry off with paper towl.
  3. Brush olive oil on both side of chicken thigh.
  4. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Place chicken in oven and cook for 35-40 minutes.
