Baked Hot Dogs

Description

Hot dogs are a type of food that typically consists of a grilled or steameed sausage served in a sliced bun. However, while less common to do so, it is more than acceptable to bake the hot dog.

Baked hot dogs are easy to assemble and pack a lot of flavor. While baking may take longer than traditional methods of cooking hot dogs there is no denying how delicious they are.

Ingredients

  • 8 hot dogs
  • 8 hot dog buns
  • 1 cup red onions
  • 1/3 cup jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup green bell pepper
  • 1/4 red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup apple
  • 1 cup shredded yellow cheese
  • 1/2 cup baked beans
  • Mustard
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Line a baking pan with foil and spray with cooking spray. Set aside for now.
  3. Heat up a skillet or pan and add olive oil
  4. When oil is hot enough add the red onion, japalepnos, bell peppers and apple. Cook on medium heat for 5 minutes and set aside.
  5. Spread a layer of mustard inside of each bun, then add hot dogs to the buns.
  6. Spread baked beans and the fried onion, peppers and apple on top of the hot dogs.
  7. Arrange hot dogs in baking pan and cover hot dogs with shredded cheese
  8. Cover baking pan with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Allow hot dogs to rest for five minutes before serving.
