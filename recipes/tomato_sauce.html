Tomato Sauce

Description

A tomato sauce consists of chopped or crushed tomatoes saut√©ed in olive oil and simmered until they lose their raw flavor. It is then seasoned to taste with meats, vegetables, herbs and spices.

A tomato sauce may be thinner than the tomato puree or tomato paste it was made from. It may serve as an ingredient in other dishes, like pastas, pizza, or tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • One 14oz can of crushed tomatos
  • 1/2 cup red onion
  • 1/3 cup jalapenos
  • 1/3 cup red bell pepper
  • 1/3 cup green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of apple
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp oregeno
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp chillie powder
  • 2 tsp of honey
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan or skillet and add the olive and seame oils.
  2. Once the pain is heated add the red onions, jalapenos, both bell peppers, apple and cook on medium high heat for 5 minutes.
  3. After the previous ingredients have had their time to cook add the can of crushed tomatoes and continue cooking on medium high heat.
  4. Once the sauce begins to bubble switch to low heat and add the Italian seasoning, oregeno, garlic powder, onion powder, chillie powder, and honey.
  5. Stir the ingredients together slowly and then cover the pan or skillet/
  6. The longer you allow the sauce to simmer the thicker it will get.
  7. Once sauce has reached desired thickness you may remove it and either serve ir or store it in the fridge for up to 1 week.
